Welcome

This site provides an automatic parsing of an JPL/Horizons output for an astronomical object (e.g. a comet) and its representation in Stellarium. You can directly copy and paste the result in your ssystem.ini in the data folder of stellarium.

If you have no clue what to do here, please refer to the Wiki page of Stellarium for importing comet data.
